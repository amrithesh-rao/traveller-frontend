About Us

Traveller is a leading organization with modern approach to travel and destination management. Established in 2001 by Mr.Vikram Mangalore with a single tempo traveler and now it is stepping towards success, satisfied with more than lakhs of passengers from Mangalore and other places.

We organize tours as a tour planner and trip adviser with huge experience and full of travel tips and knowledge. We will enable you to hunt the right vacation for value-for-money and meet your touring budget.

We provide taxi service and travel amenities based on luxury and economic point that suites your budget and we are guarantee that you will truly find our service is convenient more than your expectation. Transport vehicles include Innova, Tavera, Mini bus, Ac tempo traveler, Indica, Swift, Dzire, Etios, logan and Buses with 35 seater and 49 seater, luxury and deluxe coach that suites your convenience.

We arrange the tours for your holiday leisure and business purposes with a dedicative mind to assist you for your comfort. Our tourist destinations include many attractions in Karnataka and other states such as Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Tamilnadu, Goa, and Maharashtra. We also book hotel and resorts in reasonable prices.

Our tour packages include special offers for educational tours. We are pleased that we have many satisfied clients including Education sectors, IT companies, Government Organizations, Private Sector, Banking Hotels etc.

Our main motto is the “Customer Satisfaction”. Hence we focus on offering the tourists most attractive and leisure experiences of all tourist places in India.
